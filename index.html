What I Do

I work with founders and growth leaders at product-led SaaS companies. I help with the growth system and with the person running it.

I'm a growth advisor and a licensed psychotherapist with 12+ years across consulting, founding, and enterprise product, including product-led sales and hybrid GTM inside large organizations.

Most growth problems that stick around aren't just strategy problems. It's the growth lead who won't take big swings because they're afraid of being wrong, or the founder who keeps overriding their growth hire because letting go feels impossible, or product and sales clashing not over process but over trust. I work on both.

Growth Mentorship

For founders who hired a growth lead and want them to win.

I become the senior thinking partner your growth hire doesn't have yet. Regular sessions, async support, and real-time guidance on the actual work they're doing, but also the stuff that doesn't show up in a sprint board, like the weight of owning a number nobody else fully understands, making decisions when the data is unclear, or learning how to manage up without disappearing.

They level up faster, not just on the tactical side but as people who can actually carry the role.

Strategic Advisory

For founders and growth leaders. PLG strategy, upgrade paths, product-sales alignment, experimentation, pricing, growth roadmap.

I've done this across 50+ companies and at this point I know where things typically break before the team sees it. Small interventions in the right places make the difference between a growth system that compounds and one that spins. I advise on what to prioritize, what to stop doing, and what's actually blocking progress, whether that's a funnel problem, a pricing problem, or two teams that don't trust each other.
